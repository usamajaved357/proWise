How Snag AI works
Every feature explained — from setup to winning jobs.
Getting Started — 3 minutes
1
Add your Upwork profile URL
Go to Subscription → Profile URLs and paste your Upwork profile link. Both standard URLs (upwork.com/freelancers/~xxx) and custom usernames work.
2
Visit your profile once to sync
Open the URL in a browser tab. Snag AI reads your name, JSS, tier, skills, rate, earnings, employment history, education, and certifications automatically. A gold notification confirms the sync.
3
Add portfolio links manually
Go to Profiles → Portfolio → click Edit on each project → paste your App Store, Play Store, GitHub, or website links. These are inserted into every proposal automatically.
4
Open any job and click Write
The Write Proposal button appears on every Upwork job page. Snag AI generates a targeted proposal using your real profile data in seconds.
Smart Job Analysis & Connect Saver
Before writing a single word, Snag AI runs a full job analysis so you never waste a Connect on a bad-fit job.
FIT
Profile Match Score
Compares your skills, JSS, hourly rate, and tier against the job's requirements. 75%+ skill overlap is green. A severe mismatch caps your win score and shows a warning — skip the job and save your Connect.
JOB
Job Odds Score
Scores the job activity itself: time since posting, number of proposals already sent, client hire rate, and how many freelancers are actively interviewing. A job posted 30 minutes ago with 3 proposals scores far higher than one posted 3 days ago with 40.
⚠
10 Risk Flags — shown before you write
If any of these fire, Snag AI shows an alert with Skip/Write options: 50+ proposals, client rating <4★, unverified payment, $0 client spending, 0% hire rate, active interviews, payment not verified, and more. Red = clear skip. Yellow = use your judgement.
AI Proposal Writing
Snag AI writes proposals that are targeted, personal, and different every time — not generic AI filler.
✦
Uses your real profile data
Every proposal is built from your actual skills, tier, JSS, and portfolio links — not a generic template. Your portfolio URLs are automatically inserted before the sign-off.
↻
Refine without re-generating
After a proposal is generated, type a change in the refine box — "make it shorter", "add more about my React experience", "change the opening" — and click Generate again. Only what you asked for changes.
