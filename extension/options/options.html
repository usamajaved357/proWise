How to use
Get up and running in 3 minutes.
1
Add your profile URL
Go to Subscription tab → paste your Upwork profile URL. Snag AI will only read data from registered profiles.
2
Visit your Upwork profile
Open the URL you registered. Snag AI reads your name, JSS, tier, skills, employment history, education and certifications automatically.
3
Add portfolio links
Go to Profiles tab → expand each portfolio item → add your App Store, Play Store or website links. These are included automatically in proposals.
4
Open any job and click Write
Snag AI uses your real profile data — skills, tier, portfolio — to write a targeted proposal and calculate your win probability.
