How to use
Get up and running in under 3 minutes.
1
Fill in your profile
Add your name, skills, hourly rate, portfolio, and Upwork stats once. The more detail, the better the proposals and win probability scores.
2
Open any Upwork job
Browse Upwork normally. The Snag AI button appears automatically in the bottom-right corner of every job page.
3
Click once — done in 10 seconds
Snag AI reads the job, picks the best hook from 7 proven patterns, and writes a personalised proposal. Win probability is shown instantly.
4
Refine, copy, submit
Type a refinement instruction (e.g. "make it shorter") and hit Rewrite. Copy and paste directly into Upwork.
Pro tip: Add real portfolio links with descriptions and skills. Snag AI matches them to each job automatically and includes only the most relevant ones.
