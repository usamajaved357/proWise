How Snag AI works
Everything you need — from first setup to writing proposals that win. Read this once, apply it every day.
Quick Start
Up and running in under 3 minutes
1
Add your Upwork profile URL
Go to Subscription and paste your Upwork profile link. Both standard URLs (upwork.com/freelancers/~xxx) and custom usernames work. Click Sync to open it.
→ Subscription page
2
Visit your profile once to sync
Snag AI reads your name, JSS, tier, rate, skills, earnings, and employment history automatically when the page loads. A gold toast confirms the sync.
→ Auto on your Upwork profile
3
Sync your portfolios
In Profiles → Portfolio click ⟳ Sync portfolios. Snag AI opens your profile, reads every project's title, skills, and links automatically. Then add URLs to any project missing them.
→ Profiles page
4
Add your email & open any job
Go to Settings → Account Email and enter the same email you used to subscribe on Paddle. Then open any Upwork job and click Write Proposal.
→ Settings page
Profile & Portfolio Setup
The foundation of every strong proposal
🔗
What gets auto-synced from Upwork
When you visit your profile URL, Snag AI reads: full name, professional title, JSS %, hourly rate, tier badge (Top Rated / Rising Talent etc.), total earnings, jobs completed, hours worked, skills list, bio, employment history, education, and languages. All of this feeds into every proposal.
🗂️
Portfolio sync — must be done manually Important
Portfolios are not read during the passive profile sync. You must click ⟳ Sync portfolios in the Profiles page. This opens your Upwork profile and reads each portfolio item one by one — title, description, skills, and links. Takes 30–60 seconds.

After syncing, add or verify the URLs for each project (App Store, Play Store, GitHub, live demo). Snag AI uses these links inside every proposal as proof of your work.
⭐
Multiple profiles Pro & Agency
Pro supports up to 3 Upwork profiles. Agency supports 5. Add each profile URL in the Subscription page. In Profiles, click Set primary on the profile you want to use as the default. You can switch the primary at any time.
⚠️
Re-sync after Upwork profile changes. If you update your title, skills, tier, or add new portfolio items on Upwork, click Sync again so Snag AI picks up the latest data. Proposals use whatever was last synced.
Subscription & Billing
How your plan and usage work
🔑
Your subscription email must match your Paddle email. After purchasing a plan, go to Settings → Account Email and enter the exact same email address. This is how Snag AI verifies your active plan and unlocks your proposal quota. If the emails don't match, you'll show as free.
📅
When your proposals reset
Your quota resets on your exact subscription anniversary — not the 1st of each month. If you subscribed on June 15, proposals reset on July 15. The exact reset date is shown in Subscription → Next billing date.
⬆️
Upgrading or downgrading your plan
Go to Subscription → Upgrade your plan and click a different plan. If you already have an active subscription, Snag AI modifies your existing subscription — no new subscription is created. Paddle charges or credits the prorated difference immediately.
🚫
Cancellation — you keep access until period ends
If you cancel, your plan stays active until your current billing period ends. You won't be charged again and keep your full quota until the end of the period. The billing card shows an amber "Cancels [date]" status. After that you revert to the free plan (2 proposals).
🏦
Manage billing, update card, view invoices
Click Manage billing → in the Subscription page. This opens your Paddle customer portal where you can update your payment method, view invoices, and reactivate a canceled subscription.
Smart Job Analysis & Connect Saver
Never waste a Connect on a bad-fit job again
📊
Win Probability Score
Scores the job activity: time since posting, number of proposals already sent, client hire rate, and how many freelancers are actively interviewing. A job posted 30 minutes ago with 3 proposals scores far higher than one posted 3 days ago with 40.
🎯
Profile Match Score
Compares your skills, JSS, hourly rate, and tier against the job's requirements. 75%+ skill overlap is green. A severe mismatch caps your win score and shows a warning. This is why keeping your skills list synced and accurate matters.

AI Proposal Writing
Targeted, personal, different every time
✦
7 Opening Hooks — selected per job
Every proposal opens with one of 7 proven hook patterns. Snag AI picks the best one based on the job type, budget, timeline, and client signals — not randomly.
Hook 1 — Proof
Specific result you shipped
"I built X for [client type]. I'd like to do the same for you." Uses your top portfolio project.
Hook 2 — Relatability
I've done this exact thing
"I've shipped 4 Flutter apps to both stores. Here's how I'd approach yours:" Good for ongoing roles.
Hook 3 — Guarantee
Price + timeline up front
"I can deliver X in 2 weeks at $1,500, backed by 4 live apps." Used on fixed-price jobs.
Hook 4 — Extra Value
I'll do more than asked
"I'll not only build your features — I'll also set up crash monitoring." Differentiates from the crowd.
Hook 5 — Call
Jump on a call today
"Let's jump on a quick call today — I'll walk you through my exact approach in 15 minutes."
Hook 6 — Numbers
Lead with your stats
"Here are my numbers: 4 apps shipped, 100% JSS, 30+ hrs/week available."
Hook 7 — Client First
Restate their need better than they wrote it
"My understanding: you need a developer who owns the full mobile lifecycle — not just code, but store submissions, crash monitoring, and AI workflows." For large-budget jobs.
🔍
Screening keywords — detected automatically
Many clients hide a test phrase in the job description like "include the word 'pineapple' in your reply". Snag AI detects these patterns and includes the exact word naturally. You never miss a screening test.
↻
Refine without re-generating the full proposal
After a proposal is ready, use the quick chips — Shorter, More confident, Add my rate, Adjust for budget — or type your own instruction. Press ⌘ Enter or click Regenerate. Only what you asked changes.
❓
Client Q&A answers included
If the job has screening questions, Snag AI writes thoughtful 2–4 sentence answers in the right panel. Copy them directly into Upwork's question fields. Written in your voice, not AI-generic.
✏️
Live editing before you copy
The proposal text box is fully editable. Make any quick changes directly before copying. The word count indicator turns amber above 120 words and red above 160 to keep proposals tight.
Tips for Winning More Jobs
What actually makes the difference
⚡
Apply within 30 minutes
Being among the first 5 proposals dramatically increases your chances. Check the "Posted X minutes ago" indicator — jobs under 1 hour score highest.
🔗
Keep portfolio URLs updated
Proposals without real links rarely convert. After syncing, open each portfolio item and add the live URL — App Store, GitHub, demo site. These are inserted automatically.
📏
Shorter usually wins
Clients read the first 160 characters before deciding to expand. Keep proposals under 130 words. Use the Shorter chip if a proposal runs long.
🎯
Refine for the specific client
Use the refinement box to mention something specific from the job — "mention their FarmOS requirement" or "reference their May 28 deadline". Personal beats generic.
🚩
Respect the red flags
Clients with 0% hire rate or unverified payment almost never hire. Those Connects are real money — skip and spend them on a job that will respond.
💼
Your tier badge matters
Top Rated and Rising Talent badges appear in every proposal. Make sure your Upwork profile reflects your current tier and re-sync so Snag AI includes it.
🏆
The compound effect: Apply early + strong hook + real portfolio links + tight word count = consistently higher response rates. Each element alone helps — all four together is how you snag jobs before anyone else.
