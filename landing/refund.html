Refund Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026
Our order process is conducted by our online reseller Paddle.com. Paddle is the Merchant of Record for all our orders and handles all refund requests. To request a refund, visit paddle.net.

1. Free Trial

Snag AI offers 2 free proposals with no payment required. We strongly encourage you to test the service before subscribing. No refunds are necessary for the free tier as no payment is taken.

2. Refund Eligibility

Unused subscriptions: If you subscribed but have not generated any proposals, you may request a full refund within 3 days of your purchase date.

Once proposals have been generated: Subscriptions are non-refundable once the service has been used, as the digital content has been delivered and consumed.

This is consistent with Paddle's policy that digital content which is immediately delivered and used may be non-refundable once accessed.

3. 30-Day Guarantee

In accordance with Paddle's seller requirements, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for first-time subscribers who are genuinely dissatisfied with the service and have used fewer than 10 proposals. This is evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

To request a refund under this guarantee, email support@snagai.io within 30 days of your first subscription payment with your reason.

4. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • Subscription renewals (subsequent monthly payments after the first)
  • Requests made after 30 days of the purchase date
  • Cases where the service was actively used (more than 10 proposals generated)
  • Change of mind after heavy usage of the service
  • Dissatisfaction with Upwork outcomes — Snag AI generates proposals but cannot guarantee job wins

5. Cancellation vs Refund

Cancelling your subscription stops future billing but does not trigger a refund for the current period. You retain access to your plan until the end of the billing period.

To cancel, use your Paddle billing portal linked in your subscription confirmation email.

6. Technical Issues

If Snag AI experienced a technical failure that prevented you from using the service during your paid period, contact us at support@snagai.io and we will review your case and issue a credit or refund as appropriate.

7. How to Request a Refund

  • Visit paddle.net to contact Paddle's support team directly
  • Or email us at support@snagai.io with your order details
  • Include your email address and reason for the refund request
  • Refunds are typically processed within 5–10 business days

8. Contact

For refund questions, email support@snagai.io
