The hook system

7 proven opening hooks.

The first 160 characters of a proposal decide if a client reads on. Snag AI picks the right hook automatically based on the job type, budget, and client signals.

Hook 01
Proof of Success
"I shipped [specific result] for [client type]. I'd like to do the same for you." Uses your top matched portfolio project.
Hook 02
Relatability
"I've done exactly this before. Here's how I'd approach yours:" Great for long-term and ongoing roles.
Hook 03
Guarantee
"I can deliver X in Y timeframe at $Z — backed by 4 live apps." Used automatically on fixed-price jobs.
Hook 04
Extra Value
"I'll not only build your features — I'll also set up crash monitoring and update workflows."
Hook 05
Quick Call
"Let's jump on a call today — I'll walk you through my exact approach in 15 minutes."
Hook 06
By the Numbers
"Here are my numbers: 4 apps shipped, 100% JSS, 30+ hrs available." Used when your profile stats are strong.
Hook 07
Client First
"My understanding: you need a developer who owns the full lifecycle — not just code." For large-budget jobs.
