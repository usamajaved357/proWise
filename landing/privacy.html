Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Snag AI ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Chrome extension and website.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Email address (used to identify your account and subscription)
  • Profile data stored locally in your browser: name, title, skills, hourly rate, portfolio links, and extra context
  • Proposal settings: tone, length, always-include text

Information collected automatically:

  • Anonymous usage count (number of proposals generated) stored against your email or anonymous ID
  • Subscription plan and billing status (managed by Paddle)
  • Job page content from Upwork (title, description, skills, budget) — read only when you click Write Proposal, never stored

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalised proposals using the job data and your profile
  • To track usage against your subscription limit
  • To verify your subscription status when you open the extension
  • To send transactional emails (subscription confirmation, plan activation) via Resend

3. What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not store the content of Upwork job pages you visit
  • We do not store the proposals generated — they exist only in your browser
  • We do not use your data for advertising
  • We do not read your Upwork messages, applications, or account data

4. Data Storage

Your profile data (name, skills, settings) is stored locally in Chrome's sync storage — it stays in your browser and syncs across your Chrome devices if you're signed into Chrome.

Your email address, subscription plan, and usage count are stored in our secure database hosted on Supabase (PostgreSQL). This data is protected by industry-standard encryption.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Paddle — Payment processing and subscription management. Paddle is the Merchant of Record. See Paddle's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI proposal generation. Job content and your profile are sent to Anthropic's API to generate proposals. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase — Database hosting. See Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • Resend — Transactional email delivery. See Resend's Privacy Policy.

6. Chrome Extension Permissions

Snag AI requests the following permissions:

  • activeTab / upwork.com — To read job descriptions on Upwork pages when you click Write Proposal
  • storage — To save your profile and settings locally in Chrome

We do not request access to your browsing history, other websites, or any data beyond what is described above.

7. Data Retention

We retain your email, plan, and usage data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by emailing support@snagai.io. We will delete your data within 30 days of your request.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@snagai.io.

9. Children's Privacy

Snag AI is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via email.

11. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at support@snagai.io
